Chale Island Excursion

Chale Island

Chale island is a private island in Diani Beach that hosts the Sands at Chale Island Beach Resort & Spa. With its amazing environment, Chale Island offers amazing activities that can be enjoyed in a day tour from Diani Beach. In this trip you will have a memorable speed-boat tour to Chale Island for activities like: swimming, snorkelling, bird watching and board walking. Chale Island is one of the most popular holiday destinations in Africa for beach weddings, honeymoons, large family holidays, or for special events, exclusive business meetings and celebrations. Chale Island is a 1.2 kilometers long and 0.8 kilometers wide tropical paradise surrounded by stunning turquoise Indian Ocean and inland mangrove forest lakes subjected to the natural changes of the tides, considered to be one of the most unique treasures. The picturesque shoreline is included among the best beaches in Kenya. The views on inland mangrove lakes to the West and open Indian Ocean to the East are breathtaking! It has a fine sandy beach with much sea weed witnessed every morning due to high tide.

We will pick you up from your hotel at 08.30am and drive to the sand at Nomad beach resort. You will picked on the mainland with a speed boat to Chale Island. Enjoy snorkeling at chale accompanied by a professional guide. You will have lunch at Sands at Chale Island Beach Resort & Spa.This is the true paradise for those who are looking for an escape from it all. After Lunch enjoy swimming or sun-bathing at the hotels swimming pool. The journey back to Diani mainland will start in the afternoon.
