Amboseli National Park

Deep diving is one of the e is a 90% chance of seeing reef sharks plus the biggest fish in the sea, the whale shark. Whale sharks are particularly impressive as they cruise slowly through the water feeding their 10 meter bodies with the plentiful plankton. The Water visibility is good and the Temperatures never go below 25°c and in the summer can reach up to 30°c . This combination provides an exquisite diving experience. All dives are made by boat on a 15-50 minutes transfer to the diving sites suitable for all levels of diver experience.

  • Max: 3 Persons
  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View
  • Bed: 1

The package comes complete with equipment hire and diving guide, all you might need to Carry is a good photo and video water proof camera for the purposes of taking home some great experiences. It is alson inclusive of pick up services from any point you preffer around diani.

Tsavo National Park

This is a full day trip where you get to enjoy a morning road trip, sailng on a dhow and snorkeling. It starts at 7:30am by Pick up at your hotel, resort, cottage or villa then drive for 1hrs to Shimoni village. You will have the opportunity to see rural villages, sugar cane plantations, tall palm trees and lush greenery during the drive. After chekin in with KWS, you’ll head to the floating jetty and board a traditional Swahili Dhow for a cruise to the Marine Park.

  • Max: 3 Persons
  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View
  • Bed: 1

As you cruise through the Indian Ocean, there is an approximately 90% chance to spot Dolphins. You will have 2 or more hours of snorkeling and marvel at the rich marine life where corals, exotic fish and sea plants are protected. You are provided with masks and snorkels and are accompanied by a certified and experienced guide. Assistance is also provided to first timers. After snorkeling you will sail to wasini Island for lunch. The dish searved is mainly seafood accompanied by rice or pasta. After enjoying your lunch your boat crew will direct you back to the boat where you will sai back to shimoni. During the return period from wasini to shimoni you will be entertained by the boat crew with welcoming songs and you are invited to join the band.

Saltlick

For those who prefer to explore the ocean while dry should check out this package where you will use a boats equipped with a glass bottom to obtain an insight into the amazing world underwater. The duration of glass bottom boat excursion with snorkeling from Diani Beach is 3-4 hours, during which you can explore the magical underwater world from the comfort of the glass bottom boat.

  • Max: 3 Persons
  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View
  • Bed: 1

This excursion is mostly done during low tide to be able to explore the Robinson Island sand bank which pops up during low tides. Snorkeling can be done for swimmers but if you chose to stay on the boat or laze on the sand bank this is also perfectly fine.

  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View

The tour depends on your preference as it can be a full-day trip or just for hours however, it is only available for 4pax When you arrive at chale you will be received for a special treat by the sand at chale resort who will be your host for the day. Once at chale its all about fun as everythis is inclusive in the packace.

Watamu Sea Resort

Slowly cruise upstream on a traditional canoe or by using a Kayak as you do birdwatching among the mangroves and enjoy the serene surroundings.

  • Max: 3 Persons
  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View

This excursion takes approximately 30-45 minutes. And it is best done in the evening to be able to enjoy the magical view of the sun as it sets down.
