Maasai Mara National Park

Kenya has the most stunning beaches in the world. It also has the finest game viewing. Take a break from your coastal beach holiday and have a close encounter with the big 5 at the famous maasai mara national researve Kongoriver Safaris will transfer you to Diani airport for your morning flight. We’ll handle all the arrangements and get you from surf to safari in a quick and easy 2-hour trip. Along your flight route you’ll have views of the Great Rift Valley and towering Mt. Kilimanjaro, the highest freestanding mountain in the world. In no time you’ll touch down in the Masai Mara where you’ll be welcomed by a representative from Kongoriver Safaris who will lead your game drives over the next 3 days in a private pop up top 4 x 4 Land Cruiser Jeep.

  • Max: 3 Persons
  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View
  • Bed: 1

With its broad grasslands and distinctive acacia trees dotting the plain, the Mara is an unforgettable landscape. It’s also comfortably cool – the perfect environment for game viewing.Your Masai Mara safari features unlimited game drives and is suitable for adults, children and seniors. On day 3 there will be a pre-breakfast game drive, along with another bonus mid-morning game viewing trip. All-Inclusive. Your 3-Day Diani- Maasai Mara air safari includes roundtrip flights, park entrance fees, game drives, accommodations, meals and hotel – airport transfers. Kongoriver Safaris always offers you the most in choice and flexibility. You can even customize the cost of your safari, depending on your choice of season, the number of travelers in your party, and your pick of accommodation. Depending on your style or budget, choose from comfort or luxury safari lodges or tented camps.

Amboseli National Park

Amboseli National Park is a perfect destination for an overnight safari for those who don’t have much time. 2 Days Amboseli Safari from Nairobi takes you to Amboseli National Park where you will explore the park. Amboseli National Park is situated in Loitoktok District, Rift Valley Province of Kenya. Amboseli National Park ecosystem is mainly savannah grassland spread across Kenya -Tanzania border, an area of low scrubby vegetation and open grassy plains, all of which make easy game viewing. It is the best place in Africa to get close to free-ranging elephants among other wildlife species offering spectacular photographic experiences.

  • Max: 3 Persons
  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View
  • Bed: 1

Second in popularity after the legendary Masai Mara, the park not only offers great game viewing but is also the place to go for the best views of iconic Mount Kilimanjaro, the world’s tallest free-standing mountain. The best time to view Kilimanjaro is at dawn and sunset when the clouds lift and the light is clear and soft. The park can be accessed by road or air. Accommodation is available at several luxury safari lodges or you may do camping and you’ll explore the park on game drives, and guided nature walks. You can opt to use Kongoriver Safaris’ private 4 x 4 Land Cruiser Jeep or Customized Safari Mini-Van or landcruiser V8 for your safari.

Tsavo National Park

Tsavo East is the largest park in Kenya with many animals living there. It is the only park that is close to the coastal beaches thus can be easily reached by a Jeep or a minibus. Tsavo East National Park is one of the must-see destinations for those who want to spot animals and is characterized by its famous red earth.

  • Max: 3 Persons
  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View
  • Bed: 1

It is the classic landscape of the African savannah, it has a lower and more sparse vegetation than in Tsavo West but for this reason it is easier to spot the animals. Here you can find elephants, lions, leopards, cheetahs, giraffes, zebras, gazelles, impalas, baboons, dik-diks, caracals, buffaloes, hyenas and a wide variety of birds. Enjoy a road trip on a landcruiser jeep from Diani to tsavo east and spend a night at a lodge or camp accompanied by a local, English speaking Guide. Tsavo East national park is recommended for those who want to experience safari but have little time and is friendly for Families with children!

Saltlick

For decades Salt Lick Safari Lodge has been one of Kenya’s flagship lodges, famed for being among the World’s Most Photographed Lodges. A favourite amongst travellers in search of luxury accommodation, Salt Lick Safari Lodge is a luxurious safari escape set in the heart of the Taita Hills Wildlife Sanctuary overlooking a waterhole and the vast Tsavo plains. Salt Lick Safari Lodge’s charm is evident from the outset. Here you will savour uncompromising high standards of service, efficiency and personalised attention.

  • Max: 3 Persons
  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View
  • Bed: 1

Salt Lick Safari Lodge has 96 unique rooms, all elevated on stilts and built across two levels, with ensuite bathrooms with permanent showers and WC, and running hot and cold water. We can accommodate you in these rooms at three levels with double, twin or triple beds depending on your preferred occupancy. Whether you are here to enjoy comfort, warmth and elegance or here to celebrate life’s most important milestones you will have an unforgettable time here. Have a look through our special offers to see if there is one there perfectly made for you. The Taita Hills Wildlife Sanctuary is nestled within the Eastern Arc Range of Mountains inscribed by UNESCO as a biodiversity hotspot hosting varied endemic wildlife species and a myriad of unique flora. You will enjoy evening and morning drives connecting with the wild world of the jungle (Tsavo west)

Watamu Sea Resort

Kongo river is formed by Water from sheldrick falls in shimba hills and combines with water from other channels as it flows into the indian ocean through Kongo beach where it forms an estuary. Slowly cruise upstream on a traditional canoe or by using a Kayak as you do birdwatching among the mangroves and enjoy the serene surroundings.

  • Max: 3 Persons
  • Size: 45 m2
  • View: Sea View
  • Bed: 1

This excursion takes approximately 30-45 minutes. And it is best done in the evening to be able to enjoy the magical view of the sun as it sets down. After the trip you can enjoy drinks by the beach with your friends and even join an evening bonfire with our team till 10-11pm (this is upon request).
